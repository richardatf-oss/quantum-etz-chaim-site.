A precise model, not a metaphor

Etz Chaim as a living diagram
of physics, computation, and consciousness.

Quantum Etz Chaim treats the Tree of Life as an exact architecture: nodes as registers, paths as operations, and the descent from Keter to Malchut as the transition from infinite potential to measured reality.

This is not a replacement for physics and not a reduction of Kabbalah. It is a bridge: a shared language between sacred geometry and modern models of information and mind.
